T&H's Restaurant is the perfect venue for almost any function on Phillip Island. With it's unsurpassed view, superb menu and flexible seating, it is perfect for small, intimate occasions or grander gatherings.Function organisers are invited to develop their own menus from a selection of the best seasonal produce and ingredients. Their guests are then given a choice of main meal on the day - Alternating mains are not Harry's way, as he would rather take a bit of time and ensure each guest gets the element of choice.Ideal for weddings, birthdays, anniversaries and any other reason to celebrate, Harry's on the Esplanade offer unrivalled choice, location and service to make an event memorable.Please use the "Contact Us" button for more information on booking your next function at T&H's Restaurant.
